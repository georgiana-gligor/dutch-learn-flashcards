Lesson 4 of the course “1000 most common words in Dutch” is about food. The lesson deals with the most common types of food in Dutch. But also, typical Dutch food is discussed (such as stroopwafel, drop, pindakaas, frikandel, en hagelslag). For the Dutch learner who has not visited the Netherlands yet, this course will teach you much more than vocabulary! And even if you don’t plan to learn Dutch, this lesson about Dutch food can be very helpful to the ordinary tourist, or other visitor to the Netherlands. If there is no English menu, you now know how to say food in Dutch!

(NL) eten & drinken:::(EN) food & drinks
(NL) de groente:::(EN) the vegetables
(NL) het fruit:::(EN) the fruits

- `groente` and `fruit` are always singular
